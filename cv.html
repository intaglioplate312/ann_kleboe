Harry Bouras, University of Illinois Chicago

  • September 27, 1979
  • To whom it may concern:
  • Ann Kleboe attended my graduate seminar at the University of Illinois during the 1978-1978 acamdemic year. At the end of the seminar, it is my custom to have an extensive critique and it was at that time I became acquainted with her sculptures.
  • Miss Kleboe is an excellent stonecutter but, more than that, is able to utilize that highly intractable material to a personal end. In the year that I have known her work, I have seen it grow at a steady rate. She has certainly gone way beyond the mere exercise of her technique into the infinitely more important area of developing a personal art.
  • Sincerely,
  • Harry Bouras
